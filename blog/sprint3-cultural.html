What is Emotional Intelligence?

The ability to be aware and to understand the emotions and feelings of yourself and others and to apply them to your thoughts and actions

How is it different to IQ?

IQ (intelligence quotient) is a score which attempts to measure cognitive abilities such as memory and rational thought. IQ is quite orientated around a single individual. Comparatively, emotional intelligence is more like social intelligence and is orientated around being good at reading and interacting with multiple individuals.

Why is it important?

The majority of work is done in conjunction with others. Therefore having a high emotional intelligence will allow you to work more effectively and happily with others. It also allows you to understand yourself more which can give you a greater sense of what your goals should be and what to do.
